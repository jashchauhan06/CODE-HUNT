About Event

​Have you ever imagined building your own radar system just like the ones used in aircrafts, ships, and futuristic machines? 📡✨

​
Now’s your chance to turn imagination into reality!

​Join us for Build Your Own Radar, a hands-on workshop by Smart Tech Club (Robotics & IoT Club) where you’ll explore the exciting world of Robotics and IoT.

​In this session, you will:

  • ​Learn how to use an ESP32 microcontroller with an Ultrasonic Sensor

  • ​Create a live Radar GUI to detect and visualize objects 🎯

  • ​Connect with ThinkSpace Cloud to send and analyze real-time data ☁️

​👨‍🏫 Workshop Lead: Dr. Giridhar Urkude

​📅 Date: 22 Sept 2025

​🕒 Time: 3:00 PM – 4:40 PM

​📍 Venue: Lab 5

Whether you’re a beginner curious about IoT or a tech enthusiast ready to build something futuristic, this event will give you the perfect blend of learning + practical experience. 🚀

​✨ Get ready to step into the world of radars, signals, and cloud-powered innovation!
